Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about Student Intel

What makes Student Intel different from other student management systems?

Student Intel combines traditional student management with advanced AI-powered analytics, predictive modeling, and real-time data visualization. Unlike basic systems, we provide actionable insights that help educators identify at-risk students early, personalize learning paths, and improve institutional outcomes through data-driven decision making.

How long does implementation typically take?

Implementation time varies based on institution size and complexity. For most schools, we complete implementation within 4-8 weeks. This includes data migration, system configuration, staff training, and a pilot phase. Our implementation specialists work closely with your team to ensure a smooth transition with minimal disruption.

Is Student Intel compliant with educational data privacy regulations?

Yes, Student Intel is fully compliant with FERPA (US), GDPR (EU), COPPA, and other regional educational data protection regulations. We employ enterprise-grade encryption, role-based access controls, audit logging, and regular security assessments. Our data processing agreements ensure compliance with all applicable laws and institutional policies.

Can Student Intel integrate with our existing systems?

Yes, we offer comprehensive API integration with most common educational systems including SIS platforms, LMS systems, authentication providers, and data warehouses. Our integration specialists will work with your technical team to ensure seamless data flow between systems. We also provide pre-built connectors for popular platforms like Canvas, Blackboard, Google Classroom, and more.

What training and support do you provide after implementation?

We provide comprehensive training programs for administrators, teachers, and support staff, including live sessions, video tutorials, and detailed documentation. Post-implementation, you'll have access to our dedicated support team, regular feature updates, and ongoing professional development resources. We also offer advanced analytics training for staff who will be working with our predictive modeling tools.
