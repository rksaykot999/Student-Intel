Transforming Education Through Innovation

At Student Intel, we're pioneering the future of educational management by combining cutting-edge technology with pedagogical expertise. Our journey began with a simple vision: to empower educators and institutions with tools that make a real difference in student outcomes.

Our Story & Vision

Founded in 2022 by a team of educators and technologists, Student Intel began as a research project at Stanford University's School of Education. We recognized that while educational data was abundant, it was rarely transformed into actionable insights that could truly impact student success.

Our breakthrough came when we developed the first AI-powered predictive analytics model specifically designed for educational institutions. This technology could identify at-risk students weeks before traditional methods, allowing for timely intervention.

Today, we serve educational institutions across 35 countries, from primary schools to universities. Our platform processes over 10 million data points daily, helping educators make data-driven decisions that enhance learning outcomes.

Our Mission & Vision

Guiding principles that drive everything we do at Student Intel

Our Mission

To empower educational institutions worldwide with intelligent tools that transform data into actionable insights, enabling educators to personalize learning, identify at-risk students early, and improve overall academic outcomes through evidence-based decision making.

Our Vision

To create a world where every student receives personalized educational support, every teacher has access to predictive insights, and every institution can leverage data to create optimal learning environments that foster growth, innovation, and lifelong success.

Our Promise

We commit to continuous innovation, ethical data practices, and unwavering support for our partner institutions. We believe technology should enhance, not replace, the human connection that is fundamental to education.
